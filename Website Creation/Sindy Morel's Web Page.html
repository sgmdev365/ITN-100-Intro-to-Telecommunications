Welcome to endless possibilities of growing your own indoor plants and their benefits!

Why have indoor plants?

There are many good reasons to have plants indoors that include:

  • Improved air quality: Plants help remove pollutants and toxins from the air, making the air inside your home cleaner and healthier to breathe.
  • Reduced stress: Studies have shown that being around plants can help reduce stress levels and promote a sense of calm and relaxation.
  • Increased productivity: Having plants in your workspace or home office can help increase focus, creativity, and productivity.
  • Better mood: Being around plants can help boost your mood and reduce feelings of anxiety or depression.
  • Aesthetic appeal: Indoor plants can add a touch of natural beauty to any room, making your home feel more welcoming and comfortable.

What you will need?

    A well lit room or large window with plenty of natural light
  • Potting soil
  • Clean water
  • Cute potting containers
  • Gloves or not (it's ok to get your hands dirty!)
  • A free day (this will be too much fun to stop!)

How to get started?

  1. Put on your gloves, or not!
  2. Fill your first pot halfway with soil.
  3. Place your new plant in the pot, make sure she is centered.
  4. Add more soil to cover all the roots well.
  5. Drench your plant in water, and let the soil settle.
  6. Once all the excess water has drained, place you plant by the window.
